Hans Wilsdorf instilled a philosophy of perpetual
excellence on which the brand has forged its
reputation.


Hans Wilsdorf, founder of the company, instilled a
notion of perpetual excellence, leading to major
watchmaking innovations, such as the Oyster, the
first waterproof wristwatch and the Perpetual rotor
self-winding mechanism. The brand designs,
develops and produces the majority of its watch
components in-house. Rolex also supports the arts
and culture, sport, exploration, and those who are
devising solutions to preserve.
